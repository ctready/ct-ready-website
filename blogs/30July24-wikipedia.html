Download All of Wikipedia

30 July 2024



Did you know you can download all of Wikipedia for offline viewing, completely free? With over 6 million articles available, all you need is a hard drive and free software. Yes, the entire treasure trove of human knowledge at your fingertips, even if the internet apocalypse strikes...or more realistically if you lose internet for a couple weeks.


The Value of Information

Imagine being without the internet for days, weeks, or months. Could you handle emergencies, identify venomous snakes, or prepare for natural disasters? Wikipedia has a wealth of information for offline access, ensuring you're always prepared. Think of it as your digital survival guide when society inevitably collapses, and we all revert to bartering with canned beans.


How to Download

  1. Visit library.kiwix.org and search for Wikipedia.
  2. Choose a version: top 100 articles, simple English, full with or without pictures, or specific topic collections. It's like picking your apocalypse outfit—go simple, or go all out.
  3. Download the .zim file (size varies from under 1GB to over 100GB).

Next, download the Kiwix app to view the .zim file. Install Kiwix on your computer or store it on a hard drive for portability. I keep both Windows (.exe) and Linux (.AppImage) versions. Redundancy is key—because nothing says "prepared" like having multiple backups of your backups.


Using Kiwix

Run Kiwix and open your .zim file to access Wikipedia offline. It may be slower than the online version, but all content will be available. Be patient, even in a crisis, knowledge takes time to load.


Considerations

Wikipedia is community-edited, so verify critical information with professionals. Remember, while Wikipedia can tell you how to splint a broken leg, it can't replace a doctor. Offline versions aren't updated, so periodically re-download Wikipedia. I refresh mine annually to stay current. It’s like spring cleaning, but for your digital hoard of knowledge.


Conclusion

Having offline access to Wikipedia ensures you're prepared for any situation. It's a valuable resource that’s easy to set up and free to use. Stay informed and ready for anything—because when the zombies come, you'll want to be the one who knows how to make homemade antiseptic.
