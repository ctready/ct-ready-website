About

Hey there, I’m CT. Just a regular guy with a family, doing my best to keep us safe and sound, no matter how crazy the world gets. This blog is your go-to for straightforward, no-nonsense tips on surviving emergencies—because who needs special skills when you’ve got common sense? While we rarely face more than a few days of chaos in the U.S. before officials swoop in, I’m here to help you prep for a full 30 days of “what-the-heck-is-happening” scenarios. Want to swap survival stories or just chat? Hit me up on social media or shoot me an email—I’m always up for a good conversation.
